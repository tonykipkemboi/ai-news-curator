**Distributed Training & Optimization:**

*   **Heterogeneous Low-Bandwidth Pre-Training of LLMs (SparseLoCo):** Allows LLM training on lower-bandwidth clusters using infrequent synchronization and sparse pseudo-gradient exchange.
*   **Placement Semantics for Distributed Deep Learning:** Provides a framework for analyzing parallelism strategies in distributed deep learning based on training state placement across devices.
*   **Power-of-Two Quantization-Aware-Training (PoT-QAT) in Large Language Models (LLMs):** Reduces memory requirements and improves computational efficiency by quantizing weights to powers of two.

**Reasoning & Performance:**

*   **Falcon-H1R: Pushing the Reasoning Frontiers with a Hybrid Model for Efficient Test-Time Scaling:** Utilizes a hybrid Transformer-Mamba architecture and reinforcement learning to achieve efficient and scalable reasoning.

**Safety & Ethics:**

*   **Robust Persona-Aware Toxicity Detection with Prompt Optimization and Learned Ensembling:** Improves robustness and fairness in toxicity detection by optimizing prompts and using an SVM meta-ensemble.
*   **LLM safety guardrails/alerting**: An LLM safety guardrail and alerting tool can solve many problems regarding LLMs reinforcing grandiose/narcissistic thinking and encouraging users to spread misinformation.

**Specialized Applications:**

*   **DARC: Drum accompaniment generation with fine-grained rhythm control:** Provides fine-grained control over drum patterns using NMF and parameter-efficient fine-tuning of STAGE.
*   **Meta-Learning Guided Pruning for Few-Shot Plant Pathology on Edge Devices:** Enables real-time, on-device plant disease detection.
*   **TopoLoRA-SAM: Topology-Aware Parameter-Efficient Adaptation of Foundation Segmenters:** Adapts foundation segmentation models while preserving topology for specialized domains.
*   **POSEIDON: Physics-Optimized Seismic Energy Inference and Detection Operating Network:** Combines machine learning with geophysical laws for seismic event prediction.

**Data & Tooling:**

*   **pdfQA: Diverse, Challenging, and Realistic Question Answering over PDFs:** Introduces a new dataset for question answering over PDF documents.
