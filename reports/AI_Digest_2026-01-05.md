## 2. üî• The 1% Signal (Must-Read)
**Geometry of Reason: Spectral Signatures of Valid Mathematical Reasoning** stands out due to its training-free approach to LLM verification. By analyzing the spectral properties of attention graphs, this method can detect incoherent reasoning without requiring expensive labeled data or retraining. This capability to monitor reasoning directly improves the reliability of LLMs, aids hallucination detection, and offers architectural insights into how different attention mechanisms impact model behavior. The architectural impact comes from its potential as a real-time safety net integrated into any LLM, enhancing trust and safety in critical applications, though computational profiling will be necessary for performance optimization.
