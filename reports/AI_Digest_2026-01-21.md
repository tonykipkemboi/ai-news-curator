## 2. üî• The 1% Signal (Must-Read)
"HALT: Hallucination Assessment via Latent Testing" introduces a lightweight residual probe for near-instantaneous hallucination risk estimation in LLMs by reading intermediate hidden states. This is significant because it moves beyond token generation for hallucination detection. The probe's cost is orders of magnitude cheaper and enables fast selective generation and routing, drastically improving the reliability and trustworthiness of LLM outputs in critical applications. Architecturally, it suggests a move towards incorporating real-time risk assessment directly into the LLM inference pipeline.
