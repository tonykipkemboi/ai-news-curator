**Reasoning and Planning:**
*   **InftyThink+:** A reinforcement learning framework optimizes iterative reasoning in LLMs by learning when to summarize intermediate thoughts, what information to preserve, and how to continue reasoning. It improves accuracy, reduces latency, and generalizes better compared to chain-of-thought RL. (arXiv)
*   **When RL Meets Adaptive Speculative Training:** Proposes a unified training-serving system using reinforcement learning to adaptively train the speculator model during speculative decoding in LLMs. (arXiv)
*   **Cochain Perspectives on Temporal-Difference Signals for Learning Beyond Markov Dynamics:** Improves RL performance in non-Markovian environments by using a topological viewpoint on temporal-difference learning. (arXiv)

**World Models and Robotics:**
*   **DreamDojo:** A foundation world model for robotics trained on 44k hours of egocentric human videos. It uses continuous latent actions for knowledge transfer and demonstrates an understanding of physics and action controllability. (arXiv)
*   **From Kepler to Newton:** Introduces inductive biases (spatial smoothness, stability, and temporal locality) to enable Transformers to learn world models capturing Newtonian force representations. (arXiv)

**Security and Robustness:**
*   **Endogenous Resistance to Activation Steering in Language Models:** Investigates the phenomenon of Endogenous Steering Resistance (ESR), where large language models can resist task-misaligned activation steering during inference. (arXiv)
*   **Robustness Beyond Known Groups with Low-rank Adaptation:** Explores methods for improving the robustness of deep learning models to systematic failures on subpopulations using low-rank adaptation. (arXiv)

**Audio and Rendering:**
*   **Reciprocal Latent Fields for Precomputed Sound Propagation:** A memory-efficient framework for encoding and predicting acoustic parameters for realistic sound propagation in virtual environments, ensuring acoustic reciprocity. (arXiv)
