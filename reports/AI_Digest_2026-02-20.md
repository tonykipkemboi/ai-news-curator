## 2. 🔥 The 1% Signal (Must-Read)
The paper on "Stable Asynchrony: Variance-Controlled Off-Policy RL for LLMs" introduces a method (VCPO) to stabilize asynchronous RL training for LLMs. This is architecturally significant because asynchronous RL decouples data generation and learning, potentially leading to substantial throughput improvements. VCPO scales the learning rate based on effective sample size and uses a minimum-variance baseline, avoiding the need for an auxiliary value model and significantly stabilizing the training process, paving the way for faster and more efficient LLM finetuning in production. This has direct implications for reducing costs and accelerating the development of next-generation LLMs.
