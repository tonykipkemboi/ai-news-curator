The "Agent Contracts" paper introduces a formal framework for resource governance in autonomous AI agents, crucial for production deployment. The framework defines contracts with input/output specifications, resource constraints, temporal boundaries, and success criteria, enabling resource management and cost control. This allows predictable, auditable, and resource-bounded AI deployment with composable coordination patterns and budget discipline, preventing runaway agents and unexpected API costs. The ability to specify and enforce resource limits directly addresses concerns of uncontrolled token consumption and unpredictable behavior in production AI systems.

*   **MemRec: Collaborative Memory-Augmented Agentic Recommender System**: Introduces a decoupled agentic recommender system with a collaborative memory graph, improving recommendation quality and scalability.
